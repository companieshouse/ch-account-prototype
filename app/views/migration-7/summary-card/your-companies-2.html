Restore your digital authorisation

To file online, you'll need to enter the authentication code for each company that has a 'not authorised' status. This will restore your digital authorisation. We'll then store the code, so that you're digitally authorised.

If you do not have the authentication code, you'll either need someone who is digitally authorised to add you, or request the authentication code (opens in new tab).

Saved companies

You can view your saved companies here. You need to go to your Companies House account if you want to:

  • view and manage people who are digitally authorised
  • accept or decline invitations to be digitally authorised
  • remove a company

To do these tasks, go to the ‘Your companies’ section of your Companies House account (opens in new tab).
