Connecting existing accounts

If you have an existing WebFiling or Companies House account, or both, we'll ask you to connect them to your GOV.UK One Login (if you haven't done so already).

To connect the accounts:

  1. Create or sign in to your GOV.UK One Login on the next screen.
  2. We may ask you to enter your Companies House account password.
  3. We'll ask you to enter a security code that we'll send to your WebFiling email address.

You'll only need to do this once, so that you can keep the existing information in your accounts.

! Warning You will lose any existing information in these accounts if they do not all use the same email address. You must also use the same email address for your GOV.UK One Login.
What to do if you already have existing accounts or a GOV.UK One Login that use different email addresses to sign in

If you want to connect your accounts and keep existing information, and they use different email addresses to sign in, you'll need to update them so they're all the same. You should do this before you continue.

You'll need to sign in to each account. It is up to you which email addresses you update:

  • update your WebFiling email address
  • update your Companies House account email address (opens in a new tab)
  • update your GOV.UK One Login email address (opens in a new tab)

Then come back to this page and continue to sign in.

What to do if you share access to your accounts with other people

Only one person can connect each account to their GOV.UK One Login.

Anyone else who shares access to these accounts will need to create their own GOV.UK One Login, using a different email address. They will no longer be able to access the information in the accounts.
