What to do if you already have a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address

If you want to connect this WebFiling account to a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address, you have 2 options:

Option 1

Change your WebFiling email address to match your GOV.UK One Login email address. Then come back to WebFiling and sign in.

Option 2

Change your GOV.UK One Login email address (opens in a new tab) to match your WebFiling email address. Then come back to WebFiling and sign in.

What to do if you share access to this WebFiling account with other people

Only one person can connect this WebFiling account to their GOV.UK One Login.

Anyone else who shares access to this account will need to create their own GOV.UK One Login, using a different email address. They will no longer be able to access the information in this account.
