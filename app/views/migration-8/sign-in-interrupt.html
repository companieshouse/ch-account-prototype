Connecting existing accounts

If you have an existing WebFiling or Companies House account, or both, we'll connect them to your GOV.UK One Login (unless you've done so already).

To connect the accounts:

  1. Create or sign in to your GOV.UK One Login on the next screen.
  2. We'll ask you to enter a security code that we'll send to your email address.

You'll only need to do this once, so that you can keep the existing information in your accounts.

! Warning You should use the same email address to create your GOV.UK One Login that you use for your Webfiling account. This is so you keep the existing information in your account.
What to do if you already have existing accounts or a GOV.UK One Login that use different email addresses to sign in

If you already have an existing GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address to the one you use for WebFiling, update your GOV.UK One Login email address (opens in a new tab) if you want to keep your existing information. You may need to sign in.

If you do update your Webfiling email address (opens in a new tab), any companies you've previously added to your account will need to be added again.

When you sign in, you'll need to select a link next to each company you've previously added to restore your authorisation. If you want to keep all the existing companies you've added, and you want to change your Webfiling email address, restore your authorisation first for each company. Otherwise, you'll need to add each company again.

What to do if you share access to your accounts with other people

Only one person can connect each account to their GOV.UK One Login.

Anyone else who shares access to these accounts will need to create their own GOV.UK One Login, using a different email address. They will no longer be able to access the information in the accounts.

These changes may affect the way your business currently accesses Companies House services. It is up to each business how to adapt to these changes.
