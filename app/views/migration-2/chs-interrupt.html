We need to connect your Companies House account to your GOV.UK One Login

Before we take you to WebFiling, we need to connect your Companies House account to your GOV.UK One Login.

To do this, we'll ask you to enter your Companies House account password on the next screen. You only need to do this once. It will enable us to save any existing information that's in the account that uses the email address e*****@gmail.com.

What is a Companies House account?

This is the account you can use to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as the 'Companies House service' or 'Find and update company information'
  • some of our online services - for example, 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'

It is different to a WebFiling account.
