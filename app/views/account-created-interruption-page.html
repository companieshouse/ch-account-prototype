What happens next

If you are a director of a company

You'll need to link the company(s) to your account, so that you can update company information online.

Once you have linked your company, you can authorise people to file for your company.

If you are Person of significant control (PSC) or an Authorised person to file for a company

You'll need to link the company(s) to your account, so that you can update company information online.

The directors will need to accept the request. Once the directors have accepted the request, you will be authorised to file for the company.

To link a company to your account you'll need:

  • the company number (or numbers) that you want to link your account to
  • your Companies House authentication code
