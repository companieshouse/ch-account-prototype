Restore your digital authorisation

To file online, you'll need to enter the authentication code for each company that has a 'not authorised' status. This will restore your digital authorisation. We'll then store the code, so that you're digitally authorised.

If you do not have the authentication code, you'll either need to ask someone who is digitally authorised to add you, or request the authentication code (opens in a new tab).

Saved companies

Go to Your companies (opens in a new tab) if you want to:

  • view invitations to be digitally authorised
  • view and manage people who are digitally authorised
  • remove a company
