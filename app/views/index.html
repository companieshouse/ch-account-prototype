Companies House account

Version 1

Prototype overview

The user is guided on a linear journey through the following.

  • A user can create an account.
  • Their email (and mobile for multi-factor authentication) are verified.
  • Once the user has created their account they can link one company to their account.
  • Their name has been linked to a director on the Companies House register for that company.
  • They can add authorised people to the company.
  • They can then view their user account.

The user opens the email (below) in another tab or on another device then returns to the service. The Verify your email address page automatically updates to the Verify your mobile number page (as the user has enterered their mobile number).
