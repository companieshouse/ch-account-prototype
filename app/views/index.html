Scenario 2 *Future*

A director has no authenication code. A 'digital authorisation' request is sent to the linked director to confirm they are a director of the company.

Scenario 3 *Future*

A new company has been set up, post CH account with no authentication code. Through a link in an email, the company is linked to their account.

Prototype overview

The user is guided on a linear journey through the following.

  • A user can create an account.
  • Their email address is verified.
  • The verify email page automatically updates when the user selects link in email.
  • The user can create their password and their account is created.
  • The user does not need to sign in (as in Version 1) they can now view their account directly after creating their account.
  • Once in the account they can manage their companies and add authorised people within the account.
  • Once a company is added to the account. They can then file for this company.
