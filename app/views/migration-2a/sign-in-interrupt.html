Connecting an existing WebFiling account

If you have an existing account, we'll ask you to connect it to your GOV.UK One Login (if you haven't done so already). You only need to do this once.

To connect your account, we’ll ask you to:

  • create or sign in to your GOV.UK One Login on the next screen
  • enter a security code that we'll send to your WebFiling email address

This is so you keep the existing information in your account.

! Warning You will lose any existing information in your WebFiling account if it does not use the same email address as your GOV.UK One Login.
What to do if you already have a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address

If you want to connect your WebFiling account to a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address, you have 2 options:

Option 1

Change your WebFiling account email address (opens in a new tab) to match your GOV.UK One Login email address. Then come back to this page and continue to sign in.

Option 2

Change your GOV.UK One Login email address (opens in a new tab) to match your WebFiling email address. Then come back to this page and continue to sign in.

What to do if you share access to your WebFiling account with other people

Only one person can connect a WebFiling account to their GOV.UK One Login.

Anyone else who shares access to the account will need to create their own GOV.UK One Login, using a different email address. They will no longer be able to access the information in the WebFiling account.
