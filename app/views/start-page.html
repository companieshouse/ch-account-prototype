Companies House account

A Companies House account allows you to:

  • find and update company information and file for your company online
  • link your account with any companies you are connected to
  • authorise people who can update information and file documents for your company

To create an account, you'll need to:

  • provide your mobile number and email address
  • verify either your mobile number or email address

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
