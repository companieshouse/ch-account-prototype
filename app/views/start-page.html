Create a Companies House account

Use this service to create a Companies House user account.

A Companies House account allows you to:

  • access and use WebFiling and the Companies House service
  • add people (or 'presenters') who can update information and file on behalf of your company
  • link your account with any companies you are connected to

You'll need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone
  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • to prove your identity if you are a director or person of significant control (PSC)

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
