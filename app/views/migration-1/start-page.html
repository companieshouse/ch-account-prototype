How you sign in to WebFiling has changed

From now on, you'll sign in using GOV.UK One Login. You'll be able to create a GOV.UK One Login if you do not already have one.

Read more about the changes to signing in to WebFiling.

Sign in to WebFiling

In WebFiling, you can:

  • file your company’s annual accounts and confirmation statement
  • file director and secretary information
  • sign up to get email reminders when your accounts and confirmation statement are due
  • digitally authorise people to file for your company online

About GOV.UK One Login

This is the new simple, secure way to access government digital services using the same email address and password. In the future, you’ll be able to use it to access all services on GOV.UK.

You can also use your GOV.UK One Login to access other government services.
