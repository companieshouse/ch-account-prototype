GOV.UK One Login

This is the new way to sign in to Webfiling.

GOV.UK One Login will allow you to access many government services using the same email address and password.

! Warning To save your existing WebFiling information, you must sign in to or create a GOV.UK One Login using the same email address.

Companies House accounts that use the same email address can be connected...

This will enable us to connect your account to your GOV.UK One Login.

What to do if you already have a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address

If you want to connect a Webfiling account to a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address, you have 2 options:

Option 1

Change your WebFiling account email address to match your GOV.UK One Login email address.

Option 2

Change your GOV.UK One Login email address (opens in a new tab) to match your WebFiling account email address. Then go back to the WebFiling sign in and select continue.
