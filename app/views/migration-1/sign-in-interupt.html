Connecting existing accounts

If you have an existing WebFiling or Companies House account, or both, we'll ask you to connect them to your GOV.UK One Login (if you haven't done so already). You only need to do this once.

To do this, we'll ask you to enter:

  • your Companies House account password
  • a security code that we'll send to your WebFiling email address

It will enable us to save any information you have stored in your accounts. For example, details of companies you file for, email reminders, or companies you follow.

! Warning To save existing information, each account must use the same email address. You must also sign in to or create a GOV.UK One Login using the same email address.
What to do if you already have existing accounts or a GOV.UK One Login that use different email addresses

If you want to connect your accounts and save existing information, and they use different email addresses, you'll need to update them so they're all the same.

You'll need to sign in to each account. It is up to you which email addresses you update. The following links open in a new tab:

  • update your WebFiling email address
  • update your Companies House account email address
  • update your GOV.UK One Login email address

Then come back to this page and continue to sign in.

What to do if you share access to your accounts with other people

Only one person can connect each account to their GOV.UK One Login.

Anyone else who shares access to these accounts will need to create their own GOV.UK One Login, using a different email address. They will no longer be able to access the information in the accounts.
