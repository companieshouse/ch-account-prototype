To connect your Companies House account to your GOV.UK One Login, you must use the same email address for both. You have 2 options.

Option 1

Change your Companies House account email address

  1. Sign in to your existing Companies House account (opens in a new tab).
  2. Go to 'Your details'.
  3. Change your Companies House account email address to {{data['one-login-email']}}.
  4. Then, go back to the WebFiling and sign in.

Option 2

Change your GOV.UK One Login email address

  1. Sign in to your GOV.UK One Login (opens in a new tab).
  2. Go to 'security' and change your GOV.UK One Login email address to {{ data['chs-email'] }}.
  3. Then, go back to WebFiling and sign in.
